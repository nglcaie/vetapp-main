TERMS AND CONIDTIONS

1. Set the date of appointment atleast 1 day before the appointment.

2. Cancellation

a. Cancel the appointment atleast 1 day before the set appointment date

3. 15 minute grace period will be given.

4. If the client is not able to be on time, he/she will be given two choices:

a. Rescchedule the appointment
b. Be a walk-in client and wait for your turn
This is done to respect the time allotted for the veterinarians.

5. If the client does not show up more than 3 times on the scheduled appointment

a. Account will be temporarily restricted from scheduling appointments

6. To lift restriction:

a. go to the clinic and notify the secretary and have the account unblocked

NOTE:
Each veterinarian will only have 2 hours allotted to accommodate scheduled clients per day or depending on their arrangement within the clinic.
